Frequently Asked Questions

Quick guidance on using UdharPe‚Äîfrom ordering to payments, returns, and account security.

1. What is try and buy service?
This allows the customer to try out their products when it is delivered at their doorstep and to pay only for the items, customers would like to keep. This is a paid service, which applies to most of our products. To access this service the order placed should be of a minimum of worth Rs 999. To avail of this service please mark the tick in front of "Try and Buy" while placing your order. For our new customer's terms for this service may differ. This service will not be accessible during big sale days but will be available soon after such events.
2. Why are there different prices for the same product? Is it legal?
BuyBuy cart allows independent sellers from all over the places to sell their products on this online platform. The prices of the products are only decided by the seller itself. It may be possible that the same product is being sold at different prices by different sellers. By fulfilling all the legal requirements UdharPe allows all the sellers to present their product on this online marketplace.
3. I saw a product at Rs.1200 but post clicking on the product, there are multiple prices, and the size which I want is being sold for Rs. 1500. Why is there a change in price on the product description page?
As UdharPe allows sellers to sell their products at their choice of price, it is possible that a particular style of an item is being sold at different prices by multiple sellers. The price appearing on the page may not be the lowest price of the product for that particular design. This happens as we select which seller's price will be displayed. This is done based on various parameters and price is only being considered as one such parameter. Furthermore, you can see various prices offered by different sellers for a particular design once you click on the item.
4. How will I detect fraudulent emails/calls seeking sensitive personal and confidential information?
If such calls or emails approach you to share your sensitive information like debit/credit card PIN, net ‚Äì banking or mobile banking password claiming to be one of us, we strongly request you to never do it. UdharPe never asks for such confidential information from a customer. If you have already made such a mistake then immediately inform it to an appropriate law enforcement agency. Such baits mentioned below are often used by fraudsters to claim your money: Congratulations! You are now eligible for our luxury gift card as you have been selected to be a nominee for our 'Top BuyBuy customers'. Please share your debit/credit card details. Hi, I'm calling from UdharPe. We are excited to let you know that you have just won a coupon worth Rs 5000 on your latest purchase. Please share your debit/credit card details so that the money can be transferred directly to your bank account.
5. How to cancel an order?
An order can be canceled before it is out for delivery. However, in certain cases of specific logistic partners, this is not applicable. There's an option for canceling the order under the My Orders section. If you are unable to cancel the order online, you are free to refuse it at the time of delivery.
6. What is UdharPe's shipping policy?
We always try to deliver your orders in the best condition. We are known for our on-time delivery service. Based on the amount of your cart, there might be a certain delivery charge. On an order above the threshold value, all the orders are free.
7. How to check the status of my order?
The status of your order can be checked under My orders section.
8. How to login to UdharPe?
If you are an old UdharPe customer, you can use your username/email address to login. In case you are new to UdharPe, you need to sign up with a valid email id or Phone No.
9. What are the modes of payment in UdharPe?
We support the BNPLmi payment method to provide greater benefits to the customer. However, on UdharPe, payment methods such as Cash on Delivery, credit cards, debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and gift cards are also available along with BNPLmi, but these may incur additional charges.
10. What to do if my payment fails?
We have one of the strongest and secure servers to deal with the payments made. However, if your payment fails, we suggest you first check your bank details, billing address, and other fields. If the payment gets failed at our end, we will refund the amount back in 7-10 working days after confirmation with the bank.
11. How can I apply a coupon?
We generally give coupons to our privileged customers. A coupon can be availed on orders before making the payment. The available coupons are shown during payments. Also, we can send coupons via email to our customers.
12. What is UdharPe's fair usage policy?
We generally try hard to give convenience to our clients. In any case, we have seen that a couple of customers misuse our return policy. These customers ordinarily return a large portion of the orders purchased or decide to not acknowledge our shipments. To secure the privileges of our clients, we maintain whatever authority is needed to collect the shipment charge of Rs. 99 for all requests.
13. How are the orders placed at UdharPe delivered to me?
UdharPe has a comprehensive team of delivery agents across various cities. They collect your orders from the nearby UdharPe merchant and deliver the order.
14. What is UdharPe's Return Policy?
Our return policy allows you to exchange or return the orders for various reasons. These return requests must be made within the return period for an item. The product must not be used and should be available with tags in the original condition. Upon contacting us, we will send one of our clients at your doorstep who will check the condition of the items. On a successful quality check, the amount refund request will be initiated in case of returning orders. For exchanging an order, the item must not be used and kept under the best condition. Our delivery agent will come to you with the updated item. After a quality check, the previous item will be taken back and the newer will be delivered.
15. Can we edit or modify the address after ordering?
Yes. You are allowed to modify or change the address before your order has been processed. You can change the address by clicking edit orders online.
16. After how many days will I receive my refund?
For payments through Cash on Delivery option, UPI, credit cards, debit cards, and other online payment methods, the refund amount will be credited in 7-10 working days (Bank Holidays excluded).
17. How to create a return request?
Our user-friendly website has made the service easy. Simply go to my orders and select the items to return along with the reason to return them
18. What if I forgot my username or password?
In case you lost your password, Click on the forgot password link on the login page. Fill your username or email id to recover. You will receive a link to change your password.
19. Can I get my delivery faster?
Currently, we do not have a service like this. We will let you know if we include such a service in the future. However, we assure you of the fastest online delivery.
20. What is cashback in UdharPe?
A cashback is a special discount offered on various occasions. A percentage of the amount paid by you gets refunded in your account Wallets. Cashback is credited in 3-5 days after the delivery. Cashback is only available for online modes of payment. No cashback will be given for COD orders.
21. Do you give a discount on credit cards?
We frequently introduce discount offers on different cards to benefit our customers. Offers are valid only for online payment. We have already given discounts on all the major bank credit cards. Stay in touch for tempting offers.
22. How to redeem reward points?
Reward points are given to our happy customers on shopping with us. Collect these points to avail of amazing vouchers, gift items, and other coupons.
23. What are reward points used for?
With reward points, you can avail coupons, vouchers, and other amazing offers. You can apply for UdharPe's premium membership with 50+ reward points.
24. Can I use UdharPe to send a gift?
Yes. You can always choose UdharPe's gift delivery to make your loved ones feel special. There is an option during checkout to select if you need to deliver the item as a gift. A nominal fee of Rs 25 is charged for gift wrapping.
25. Can I use social login on UdharPe?
You can connect your Facebook account with UdharPe for timely updates and amazing offers. With social login, you will easily find items based on your interest.
26. What is the BNPLmi option for buying on udhar and how does it benefit the customer?
BNPLmi is a patented technology. Using it, customers can make their entire purchase on credit for one year and earn interest by keeping the remaining amount in a bank fixed deposit (FD). This not only saves the customer money on expenses but also provides additional income in the form of interest.
27. Is there any discount on Bulk Orders?
Yes. Under our volume-tiered discount scheme, you can avail upto 15% discount on selected items. You can also contact our customer support team for more details on quantity discounts. GST benefits are also available on bulk orders.
