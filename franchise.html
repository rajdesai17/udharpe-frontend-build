About UdharPe

Our company, "UdharPe", was founded in 2025 with the sole objective of empowering women entrepreneurs and becoming a leading one-stop solution for all credit-based grocery stores in the market.

This company adheres to high-quality standards and caters to affordable needs across various categories including groceries, sports equipment, office supplies, men's and women's accessories, and much more!

"UdharPe" aims to create a success story for women entrepreneurs, enabling them to achieve a better standard of living, financial independence, and respect.

Simultaneously, it provides customers with a convenient way to purchase high-quality goods on credit from their local stores.
