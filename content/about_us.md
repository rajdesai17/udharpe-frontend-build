Who We Are
Udharpe is India's fastest-growing government-recognized startup supermarket franchise developer company, founded in 2025, with plans to launch over 1000 women-led stores nationwide. The company empowers local women by training and enabling them to open smart, technology-enabled UDHAR grocery outlets. Udharpe operates on a unique zero franchise fee and zero royalty fee model, making it easy for women entrepreneurs to start their smart UDHAR grocery business with minimal investment.
Key features include patented technology for secure udhar sales and its recovery, an advanced POS system, mobile app integration for online ordering, inventory support, employee training, and operational guidance. This combination creates a cost-effective shopping experience for customers and rapid, sustainable sales growth for franchise partners.
Udharpe's product range will include groceries, packaged foods, personal care products, household essentials, utensils, and kitchen appliances, sourced from over 300 national and international brands. Udharpe also allows franchisees to source from local suppliers, enabling them to manage their profit margins effectively.
Udharpe's mission is to empower women to compete with large e-commerce companies by adopting modern retail secured udhar practices, thereby improving customer experience, driving rapid sales growth, and building strong local business communities. With innovative and patented technology and customer-centric services, Udharpe aims to become India's largest women-led, digitally integrated credit retail network.

FOFO Models Designed for Every Women Entrepreneur’s Needs
 
What is the FOFO Model?
FOFO stands for Franchise Owned, Franchise Operated.
•	Ownership: The franchisee invests in the outlet, infrastructure, and interiors. 
•	Operations: The franchisee independently runs the business while following the brand’s training, guidelines, support and systems. 
•	Returns: The franchisee keeps full profits, after paying a royalty or agreed share to the franchisor. 
Advantages:
•	Franchisees enjoy complete operational control. 
•	Higher profitability potential compared to FOCO. 
•	Stronger customer relationships through local ownership. 
•	Ability to adapt to local preferences and market trends.
