How we collect, safeguard, and use your data when you browse our site, place orders, and use the UdharPe mobile app.

UdharPe ("we", "our", "us") operates the UdharPe mobile application and the website www.UdharPe.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website, mobile app, and related services.

By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We may collect the following information when you register, place an order, or engage with our platform:

  • Full name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address
  • Saved addresses
  • Payment details (processed via secure third-party gateways; we do not store card/bank details)

b. Non-Personal Information

  • Device details
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics
  • Browser type
  • Cookies and analytics data

c. App Permissions

UdharPe app may request:

  • Camera (for QR scanning, document upload)
  • Location (to show nearby stores and delivery availability)
  • Storage (upload/download files)
  • Notifications (for order updates, offers)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Register and manage your UdharPe account
  • Process orders, payments & deliveries
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app functionality & user experience
  • Send offers, promotions, and important notifications
  • Prevent fraud and ensure account safety
  • Comply with legal requirements

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information.

We may share limited data with:

  • Delivery partners to complete your order
  • Payment gateways, Udhar solution providers for secure transactions
  • Customer support providers
  • Analytics and service tools for app improvement
  • Law enforcement if legally required

All partners follow strict confidentiality and data protection standards.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to safeguard your personal information.

While we take all necessary precautions, no online platform can guarantee 100% security.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Enhance browsing experience
  • Save preferences
  • Analyze traffic

You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Links

UdharPe may contain links to external websites or services.

We are not responsible for their privacy practices or policies.

7. Your Rights

You can request:

  • Access to your stored personal data
  • Corrections or updates
  • Deletion of your account
  • Restriction of certain data uses
  • Opt-out from marketing communications

Contact us anytime for these requests.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may modify or update this policy periodically.

The updated version will be posted on this page with the new "Last Updated" date.
